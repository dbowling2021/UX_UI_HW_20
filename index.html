Problem Statement



Users find it challenging to select outfits for different occasions. It has proven to be a time-consuming process and some users find it easier to buy new instead of searching through their wardrobe.




The Solution



We believe providing users with a clothing organization and recommendation app will provide them with a sense of ease and confidence when selecting outfits for an occasion based on their current wardrobe.

Competitor Analysis






Stitch Fix

Personal styling for men, women & kids that sends clothing to your door



Main Feature:

The app pairs you with a personal stylist and their fit technology to hand-select and send clothing and accessories for the user's one-of-a-kind taste and price range.





Smart Closet

Smart and easy-to-use tool to help you manage your wardrobe and daily outfit



Main Feature:

The app has a detailed data generation and statistics page that keeps track of your most used items, unused items, looks, most worn looks, Favorite brands, and many others. It even keeps track of your spending and the total value of your closet.

Usability Test



We conducted a usability test to ensure our app is easy to navigate and users can complete the app's task(s)/purpose. We hope to be made aware of any pain points and discover any improvements that can help our app succeed.






Results








What did you learn?




  • We found that our users spend entirely too much time determining what to wear.


  • We found that our users are not always comfortable with their style choice.


  • We found that our users have a hard time picking an outfit to wear based on the occassion and weather.




What are the next steps?




  • Revisit the design thinking process to guide us while iterating Pocket Stylist to make it front-facing ready for resource support and funding.


  • Build and implement the style-assist feature to provide users with on-demand access to prominent stylists to help improve their style.


  • Explore the possibility of adding a shopping feature for users to purchase clothing via Pocket Stylist.
